About

Hi, I'm Sijia Cai

I am a Ph.D. student at Department of Astronomy, Tsinghua University, working with Zheng Cai. I study high-redshift galaxies through multi-wavelength observations, with emphasis on galaxy formation, evolution, and the early universe.

High-Redshift Galaxies
